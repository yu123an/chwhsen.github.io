#### 魔改过程

首先是对_config.yml配置文件的修改

```ruby
#-------------
个人信息
#-------------
name: "第七间夜店"
permalink: /:year/:month/:day/:title.html
author: "chwhsen"
atom-baseurl: "https://chwhsen.github.io"
disqus: "yuanyuan"
#------------
菜单页
#------------
navigations:
  - title: 归档
    class: fa fa-list-ul
    href: /archive.html
  - title: 标签
    class: fa fa-tags
    href: /tags.html
  - title: Stop
    class: fa fa-user
    href: /about.html
#  - title: Gallery
#    class: fa fa-film
#    href: 
#  - title: Subscribe
#    class: fa fa-rss
#    href: /atom.xml
#-------------
百度统计
#-------------
baidu-analysis: 9c651515b864fe89150625c30c3dce34
```

> 订阅界面和画册界面我给注释掉了，用上的可能性不大

其次是/_layout里模板的微调

> 修改主要集中在两部分，页脚的个性签名，以及增加了百度统计的代码块
